About Us

Established in Late 2015, the gamer kiosk has grown into one of the most gaming community in Nairobi,Kenya.We are a multi-gaming community that supports any and every game there is a crowd for. We aim to provide for and cater to both casual and competitive gamers alike. Come hang out with us and enjoy gaming and create new friendships.

We also offer live streams for major esports events. As you cheer or get frustrated by the pro-gamers in style with a large screen + 3D sound surround, you can find solace in our wide range of cocktails.
